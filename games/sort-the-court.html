Sort the Court

4.9 (56 ratings)

Sort the Court is a delightful kingdom management game where you play as a ruler who must make decisions by simply answering "yes" or "no". Each day, various characters will visit your court with requests, problems, or opportunities.

Features:

  • Simple yet engaging gameplay
  • Charming pixel art style
  • Multiple story paths and endings
  • Quirky characters and humor
  • Resource management elements
