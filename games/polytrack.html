PolyTrack is a minimalist track building game that lets you create and optimize racing circuits. With its simple yet powerful tools, you can design the perfect racing experience.

Features:

  • Intuitive track building tools
  • Clean, minimalist design
  • Multiple track types and surfaces
  • Test your tracks in real-time
  • Share your creations with others
