About Train Planner

Train Planner is an open-ended puzzle game where you have to create a safe, efficient and pretty rail network to let Miku deliver her leeks. It's important work!

Features:

  • Create complex railway networks
  • Manage train traffic with signals
  • Beautiful pixel art graphics
  • Relaxing gameplay experience
