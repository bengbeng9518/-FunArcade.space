About Astro Prospector

Astro Prospector is an engaging space mining adventure that combines resource management with arcade-style gameplay. As a lone prospector in the vast cosmos, you'll navigate treacherous asteroid fields, mine valuable resources, and upgrade your ship to become the most successful space miner in the galaxy.

With intuitive controls and progressive difficulty, Astro Prospector offers an exciting experience for both casual and hardcore gamers. Every session presents new challenges and opportunities in the endless expanse of space.
